My first signature method, Ordered Network Analysis (ONA), has been employed in 100+ published works across fields such as quantitative ethnography, learning analytics, AI in education, healthcare, and policy analysis since its inception (ONA method paper). ONA was recognized as Best Student Paper at the 2022 International Conference on Quantitative Ethnography.

My current work, Epistemic Trajectory Modeling (ETM), extends ONA to model learning trajectories. Rather than summarizing learning as a static network, this new method traces the underlying mechanisms that drive behavior change over time and describes learning as a dynamic process as it actually unfolds.
